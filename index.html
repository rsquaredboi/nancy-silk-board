Execution

The 90-day game plan

Funnel-first, inventory-last — the same sequence that works everywhere else at Nancy: prove the angle with paid traffic before the PO, then let verified demand size the buy.

Phase 0Wk 1–2

Samples + receipts

  • Request quotes + samples from Taihu Snow, DreamSilk, Silkua: 19mm slip, robe, cami set; 22mm robe. Demand OEKO-TEX cert IDs, SGS reports, landed-cost quotes (FOB + freight + duties).
  • Burn-test / lab-verify fabric composition on arrival — Alibaba "100% silk" is frequently poly.
  • Spec the gift box (factory-made) for the After Dark unboxing.
  • Fold supplier visits into the Kings Group China trip — Suzhou is ~2h from Shanghai.
Phase 1Wk 2–5

Angle validation before inventory

  • Mine Nancy's 20K+ reviews (/review-intel) for silk/robe/gift language; run the Zigpoll survey with a silk-interest question.
  • Build 2 advertorials ("The robe that starts something" + "What 19 momme means") and 1 waitlist/preorder LP on get.officialnancy.com. Pixel 832767357702650 on everything.
  • $3–5K Meta test across the 3 concept creative worlds (statics from the generated boards + sample photography when it lands).
  • Kill criteria: sub-1% LP conversion to waitlist/preorder at target CPC = shelve; 3%+ = fast-track the PO.
Phase 2Wk 5–9

Test buy + funnel build

  • PO the winning 2 hero SKUs at 50–150 units/style (~$5–15K inventory, air freight, 20–30 day production).
  • Real-product shoot in the winning concept world; /creative-vault library (500+ statics, UGC, talking-head) off the real photography.
  • Build PDP + bundle flow on the Nancy Shopify stack; wire the Anniversary Kit as the AOV play.
  • Seed 50 units to SideShift creators for the unboxing UGC wave.
Phase 3Wk 9–13

Launch + scale into Q4

  • Full funnel live: ad → advertorial → PDP/bundle. Scale winners by 30-day rolling ROAS, 7-day trends alongside.
  • Existing-customer email/SMS drop first — cheapest revenue and the fastest review velocity.
  • Reorder deep on winners (150→500+/style) in time for the Nov gift window; expand to He-Buys-It gift quiz for Valentine's.
  • Decision gate at day 90: standalone sub-brand vs. permanent Nancy category, using LTV:CAC from the calculator.

Eyes Open

Open questions & honest risks

Competitor economics unverified

Every web claim about Lunya/SKIMS/Eberjey AOVs and funnels failed adversarial verification. Next loop: direct site teardowns + deeper ad-library pulls on US players before finalizing price points.

Set-level unit costs unquoted

The verified $7.70–10.20 FOB covers underwear/bralette sets only. Slip/robe/PJ-set costs are quote-based — margins stay provisional until Phase 0 sampling returns landed costs.

Gifting thesis lacks data

The "42% of luxury sleepwear is gifted" stat was refuted. The Anniversary Kit is still strategically sound (unique to Nancy) but validate gift demand via our own survey + the Phase 1 test before building the calendar around it.

Health-adjacent claims need substantiation

Thermoregulation/menopause and skin-benefit claims need citable support before ad copy (same discipline as Lem: modality-only claims). Until then: comfort language only.

Meta creative policy

Intimacy-led positioning must stay elegant in-feed. Lead with the robe, the box, the morning after — keep the explicit connection to Nancy's catalog on the advertorial and PDP, not the ad.

Fabric fraud

"100% mulberry silk" on Alibaba is frequently poly satin. Lab verification is a hard gate before any PO — one bad batch kills the "honest spec" brand pillar permanently.
